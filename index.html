Welcome to Cool Animal Facts!

Here at Cool Animal Facts we are dedicated to educating everyone, young or old, about cool animals! Cool animals are one of the best parts of Earth, and we are happy to teach everyone about them!


Due to a limited budget and our website developer having chronic procrastination, our website currently does not display all of the cool animals. However we have chosen the coolest animals to show because our boss needs to see that we at least did something.


After you look around the site and get a glimpse of some of the coolest animals on Earth, please consider taking the survey!
